Key Concepts Identified

Contact aggregation — Scan and pull contacts from LinkedIn, phone contacts, email, and call logs into one place
Tinder-style onboarding — Swipe left/right on contacts to quickly filter who you want to keep in touch with vs. not
Relationship intelligence — Read prior messages, emails, shared LinkedIn connections to surface people you actually have real relationships with
Relationship categorization — Personal only, business only, or personal + business (friend you also work with, networking contact, close friend, etc.)
Smart outreach prompts — Prioritized suggestions of who to reach out to, based on how long it's been and relationship type
Weekly outreach planning — "How many people do you want to contact this week? Business or personal?" followed by Tinder-style card swiping to confirm
Calendar integration — Tie into calendar to notice free time and nudge outreach
Social media summaries — Pull recent LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter activity to give conversation starters before reaching out
Call recording & transcription — Record and transcribe phone calls (like Granola), save transcripts to contact profiles
Action item extraction — Surface to-do's and follow-ups from conversations ("When you talked to so-and-so last week, they mentioned this...")
Push notifications — Reminders to reach out to specific people
Conversation history — Full log per contact so you can go back and get a summary of what you last talked about
